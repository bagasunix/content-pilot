FAQ

What AI providers are supported?

Any OpenAI-compatible API: OpenAI, DeepSeek, Ollama (local), OpenRouter, Groq, and more. You can also use custom endpoints.

Is my data safe?

Yes! Your API keys are encrypted and never stored in plain text. We don't have access to your content or AI provider.

Can I use it offline?

ContentPilot is a desktop app that requires internet for AI generation and publishing. However, you can edit existing drafts offline.

Which platforms are supported?

Currently Blogger and WordPress (via REST API). More platforms coming soon.
