# Blog Monetization (Research-Based)

## Revenue Metrics & Benchmarks

### AdSense RPM (Revenue per 1000 Sessions)
| Ad Type | Avg RPM |
|---------|---------|
| Display (sidebar) | $2-5 |
| In-article | $5-12 (best) |
| In-feed | $3-8 |
| Anchor (mobile) | $4-10 |

### Key Metrics
- **RPM**: target > $5 (tech niche)
- **CTR**: avg 1-3%
- **CPC**: varies by niche (tech: $0.5-2)
- **Page views**: need 10,000+ monthly for decent revenue

## AdSense Optimization

### Ad Placement (Research-Based)
1. **Above fold**: one ad unit, non-intrusive
2. **In-content**: after 2nd or 3rd paragraph (highest RPM)
3. **End of article**: between content and comments
4. **Mobile**: anchor + in-article combo
5. **Lazy load**: ads below fold for page speed

### Layout Best Practices
- **Auto ads**: let Google optimize (start here)
- **Manual placement**: fine-tune after data
- **Responsive ads**: adapt to screen size
- **Matched content**: increase engagement

### AdSense Policy Compliance
- **No click farming**: instant ban
- **No misleading placement**: ads must be clearly ads
- **No prohibited content**: adult, violence, drugs
- **Maintain UX**: ads < content ratio

## Affiliate Marketing

### Program Selection
- **Relevance**: must match your niche
- **Commission**: > 10% for physical, > 20% for digital
- **Cookie duration**: > 30 days preferred
- **Payment**: reliable, timely

### Placement Strategy
- **Product reviews**: natural context
- **Comparison articles**: "X vs Y"
- **Resource pages**: "Tools I Use"
- **Tutorial content**: "How to [X] with [tool]"

### Disclosure (FTC Required)
```
**Disclosure**: Artikel ini mengandung link afiliasi. Jika kamu membeli melalui link tersebut, saya mendapat komisi tanpa biaya tambahan untuk kamu.
```

## Revenue Diversification

### Tier 1: Easy (Start Here)
- **AdSense**: passive, requires traffic
- **Affiliate links**: contextual, requires trust

### Tier 2: Medium (Growth Phase)
- **Sponsored posts**: $50-500 per post
- **Digital products**: ebooks, templates
- **Email newsletter ads**: $0.01-0.05 per subscriber

### Tier 3: Advanced (Scale)
- **Online courses**: high margin
- **Consulting**: expertise monetization
- **Membership**: recurring revenue

## Pitfalls
1. **Don't sacrifice UX for ads** — bounce rate increases
2. **Don't click your own ads** — instant ban
3. **Don't hide disclosure** — FTC violation
4. **Track RPM by page** — optimize high-traffic pages first
5. **Mobile optimization** — 60%+ traffic is mobile
6. **Page speed** — lazy load ads below fold

## Verification

- Affiliate CTR > 3%
