# Human Voice Editor (Research-Based)

## Research Basis
- **Flesch Reading Ease**: target > 60 for Indonesian blog
- **Sentence length**: 15-20 words average
- **Paragraph length**: 3-5 sentences
- **One idea per paragraph**

## AI Artifacts to Remove

### Structural Artifacts
- Excessive bullet points → flowing paragraphs
- Every paragraph same length → vary naturally
- Perfect parallel structure → add natural variation
- Numbered lists for everything → use prose where appropriate

### Word Choice Artifacts
- "Dalam era digital saat ini" → make specific
- "Penting untuk diketahui" → just state the fact
- "Secara garis besar" → "Singkatnya" or cut
- "Tidak dapat dipungkiri" → remove entirely
- "Pada dasarnya" → cut

### Sentence Patterns
- **Start sentences differently**: avoid repeated "Ini adalah..."
- **Vary sentence length**: mix short and long
- **Use contractions**: "kamu" not "anda" for casual tone
- **Add personal touches**: "Saya pernah mengalami..."

## Indonesian Blog Conventions

### Tone
- **Conversational**: not formal academic
- **Direct address**: "Kamu pasti pernah..."
- **Personal anecdotes**: build trust
- **Technical terms**: keep English if standard

### Cultural Context
- **Avoid**: overly formal language
- **Use**: colloquialisms where appropriate
- **Reference**: local examples (Indonesian brands, events)
- **Balance**: professional yet approachable

## Readability Checklist

### Flesch Reading Ease Score
- **90-100**: Very easy (elementary)
- **80-89**: Easy
- **70-79**: Fairly easy
- **60-69**: Standard (target for blog)
- **50-59**: Fairly difficult
- **30-49**: Difficult

### Sentence Structure
- Average sentence length: 15-20 words
- Max sentence length: 30 words
- Mix of simple, compound, complex sentences
- Avoid consecutive short sentences

### Paragraph Structure
- 3-5 sentences per paragraph
- One main idea per paragraph
- Topic sentence first
- Supporting details follow

## Editing Workflow

### Step 1: AI Detection
- Read aloud — does it sound natural?
- Check for: parallel structure, repetition, formal tone
- Highlight: phrases that feel "generated"

### Step 2: Structural Edit
- Break bullet points into prose
- Vary paragraph lengths
- Add transitions between sections

### Step 3: Word Choice
- Replace formal words with conversational
- Add contractions
- Insert personal examples

### Step 4: Readability Check
- Calculate Flesch score
- Check sentence length distribution
- Verify paragraph structure

## Pitfalls
1. Don't remove technical accuracy for readability
2. Don't over-casualize professional content
3. Don't remove all structure — some bullets are good
4. Don't add filler just to vary sentences
5. Read final version aloud before publishing

## Verification
- Flesch score > 60
- No consecutive sentences starting same word
- Personal anecdote or example in each section
- Reads naturally when spoken aloud
