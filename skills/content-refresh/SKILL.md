# Content Refresh (Research-Backed)

## Research Data
- **HubSpot**: refreshed posts saw **106% average traffic increase**
- **Backlinko**: content updates drove **111% more organic traffic**
- **Timeline**: results visible in 2-4 weeks after refresh

## Trigger Conditions
- Traffic dropped >25% in 6 months (GA4)
- Impressions dropping but position stable (GSC)
- Content older than 12 months with declining performance
- Competitor content outranking existing article

## Decay Detection

### GA4 Method
1. Sort by: Engagement > Page views
2. Compare: Last 90 days vs previous 90 days
3. Filter: pages with >30% traffic decline
4. Exclude: seasonal content (holiday, event-specific)

### GSC Method
1. Performance > Pages
2. Sort by: impressions change (descending)
3. Identify: pages with dropping impressions
4. Cross-reference: click-through rate changes

### Rule of Thumb
- Traffic dropped >25% in 6 months = refresh candidate
- Impressions stable but CTR dropping = title/meta issue
- Position dropping = content depth/quality issue

## Refresh Framework (4-Step)

### Step 1: Audit
- Check: outdated statistics, broken links, thin sections
- Compare: your content vs top 5 SERP results
- Identify: what competitors cover that you don't
- Note: PAA questions you haven't answered

### Step 2: Update
- Refresh all statistics and data points (within 12 months)
- Fix broken external links
- Add new sections (30-50% more depth)
- Update screenshots and images
- Add internal links to new content

### Step 3: Optimize
- Update meta description with fresh angle
- Improve title (keep keyword, add freshness signal)
- Add structured data if applicable
- Check mobile rendering

### Step 4: Re-index & Promote
- Submit updated URL to GSC for re-indexing
- Share refreshed content on social media
- Include in newsletter as "Updated" article
- Track: impressions and clicks recovery

## What to Update
- Statistics and data points (must be current year)
- Screenshots and images (if showing old UI)
- Internal links (point to newest related content)
- External links (check for broken/dead links)
- Meta descriptions (fresh angle)
- Add new sections addressing PAA questions

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] All statistics updated to current year
- [ ] No broken links
- [ ] 30%+ more content depth than before
- [ ] PAA questions answered
- [ ] Internal links to 3+ related articles
- [ ] Meta description updated
- [ ] Images optimized and current
- [ ] Submitted to GSC for re-index

## Pitfalls
1. Don't just change dates — add real value
2. Don't remove content that's still ranking well
3. Don't change URL — update in place
4. Don't refresh too frequently — 6+ month gaps
5. Track results — don't refresh blind

## Verification
- Traffic recovered within 4 weeks
- Impressions increasing in GSC
- Position stable or improving
- No new crawl errors
