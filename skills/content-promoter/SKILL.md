# Content Promoter (Distribution Research)

## Research Basis
- **Social media**: drives 31% of referral traffic
- **Newsletter**: 4x higher conversion than social
- **Community**: highest engagement rates

## Distribution Channels

### Social Media (Primary)
**Twitter/X**
- **Timing**: 10 AM, 2 PM, 4 PM
- **Format**: Hook + link + hashtag
- **Frequency**: 3-5 posts per article

**LinkedIn**
- **Timing**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM
- **Format**: Professional insight + link
- **Frequency**: 1-2 posts per article

**Instagram**
- **Timing**: 11 AM, 7 PM
- **Format**: Carousel or story + link in bio
- **Frequency**: 1-2 posts per article

**WhatsApp**
- **Timing**: Evening
- **Format**: Short hook + link
- **Frequency**: 1 post per article

### Newsletter (High Conversion)
- **Timing**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM
- **Format**: Article excerpt + CTA
- **Frequency**: Include in weekly newsletter

### Community (High Engagement)
- **Reddit**: relevant subreddits
- **Facebook Groups**: niche communities
- **Quora**: answer questions with link
- **Forums**: relevant discussions

## Promotion Schedule

### Day 1: Launch
- Publish article
- Share on all social platforms
- Include in newsletter
- Submit to GSC

### Day 2-3: Follow-up
- Reshare with different hook
- Engage with comments
- Answer questions in communities

### Day 7: Recap
- Share "most popular" post
- Thank commenters
- Highlight key insights

### Day 30: Refresh
- Reshare with updated stats
- Include in monthly roundup

## Content Format Per Platform

### Twitter/X (5 variations)
1. Hook + stat + link
2. Thread: 5 key takeaways
3. Question + answer + link
4. Quote from article
5. Controversial opinion + link

### LinkedIn (2 variations)
1. Professional insight + link
2. Case study or example

### Instagram (2 variations)
1. Carousel: key points
2. Story: behind-the-scenes

## Automation Opportunities

### Tools
- **Buffer**: schedule posts
- **Hootsuite**: multi-platform
- **Later**: visual planning
- **SocialBee**: evergreen recycling

### Templates
- Platform-specific post templates
- Image templates per platform
- Hashtag research templates

## Metrics to Track

### Per Platform
- **Reach**: impressions
- **Engagement**: likes, comments, shares
- **Clicks**: link clicks
- **Conversions**: newsletter signups

### Overall
- **Referral traffic**: by channel
- **Conversion rate**: by channel
- **ROI**: time spent vs traffic

## Pitfalls
1. Don't spam — space out posts
2. Don't ignore engagement — respond to comments
3. Don't use same content for all platforms
4. Don't forget to track results
5. Don't neglect community engagement

## Verification
- All platforms posted
- Engagement tracked
- Referral traffic monitored
- Conversions measured
- Promotion schedule followed
