# Content Repurposing (Research-Based)

## Research Basis
- **HubSpot**: 60% of marketers repurpose content 2-5 times
- **Conversion**: repurposed content has 3x higher conversion
- **Efficiency**: 1 article → 10+ content pieces

## Repurposing Matrix

### From Blog Article → 10+ Pieces
1. **Social media posts** (5-10 per article)
2. **Newsletter excerpt**
3. **Infographic** (key data points)
4. **Video script** (YouTube/TikTok)
5. **Email course** (series of related articles)
6. **Ebook/PDF guide** (combine 5-10 articles)
7. **Podcast episode** (discuss key points)
8. **Twitter thread** (step-by-step)
9. **LinkedIn article** (professional angle)
10. **Quora answers** (link back to original)

### Social Media Breakdown

**Twitter/X (5-10 posts per article)**
- Hook + key stat + link
- Quote from article
- Thread: 5 key takeaways
- Question + answer + link

**LinkedIn (1-2 posts)**
- Professional insight from article
- Case study or example

**Instagram (1-2 posts)**
- Infographic carousel
- Quote image + caption

**WhatsApp (1 post)**
- Short hook + link

## Platform-Specific Formats

### Infographic
- Extract 5-10 key data points
- Use Canva or Figma template
- Include brand colors
- Share on Pinterest + social

### Video Script
- Extract intro + 3 key points + CTA
- 3-5 minute length
- Add visual demonstrations
- Include subtitles

### Email Newsletter
- Summarize article in 3-5 sentences
- Add personal angle
- Include clear CTA
- Link to full article

### Ebook/PDF
- Combine 5-10 related articles
- Add introduction + conclusion
- Include table of contents
- Design with brand template

## Automation Opportunities

### Batch Repurposing
1. Write one comprehensive article (2000+ words)
2. Extract all repurposing elements
3. Schedule social posts (Buffer, Hootsuite)
4. Add newsletter section
5. Track: each repurpose → traffic to original

### Template System
- Social post templates per platform
- Infographic templates per content type
- Email templates for newsletter sections
- Video script outlines

## Pitfalls
1. Don't just copy-paste — adapt for each platform
2. Don't repurpose everything — quality > quantity
3. Don't forget to link back to original
4. Don't ignore platform-specific best practices
5. Track which repurposing drives most traffic

## Verification
- At least 5 repurposed pieces per article
- Each repurposed piece links back to original
- Platform-specific formatting applied
- Performance tracked (clicks, engagement)
