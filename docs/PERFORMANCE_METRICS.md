# Performance Metrics Framework

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Content Output Metrics
| Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Articles Published | 2-3/week | Weekly | Orchestrator |
| Research Briefs | 3-4/week | Weekly | Researcher |
| Drafts Completed | 2-3/week | Weekly | Writer |
| Reviews Completed | 2-3/week | Weekly | Editor |
| Quality Reports | 2-3/week | Weekly | Analyst |

### Quality Metrics
| Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Content Quality Score | 85+ | Per article | Analyst |
| SEO Score | 80+ | Per article | Analyst |
| Grammar Score | 95+ | Per article | Editor |
| Readability Score | Grade 8-10 | Per article | Editor |

### Pipeline Efficiency Metrics
| Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Idea to Brief | 24 hours | Per article | Researcher |
| Brief to Draft | 48 hours | Per article | Writer |
| Draft to Review | 24 hours | Per article | Editor |
| Review to Publish | 24 hours | Per article | Orchestrator |
| Total Pipeline Time | 5-7 days | Per article | Orchestrator |

### Team Performance Metrics
| Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Task Completion Rate | 95% | Weekly | Orchestrator |
| Rework Rate | <10% | Weekly | All |
| Response Time | <2 hours | Daily | All |
| Quality Consistency | 90%+ | Weekly | Orchestrator |

### Audience Engagement Metrics
| Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Page Views | +10% monthly | Monthly | Analyst |
| Time on Page | 3+ minutes | Per article | Analyst |
| Bounce Rate | <50% | Per article | Analyst |
| Social Shares | 10+ | Per article | Analyst |
| Comments | 5+ | Per article | Analyst |

## Weekly Performance Review

### Monday: Planning & Status
- Review previous week's metrics
- Identify bottlenecks
- Plan new content
- Set priorities

### Wednesday: Progress Check
- Track pipeline progress
- Address any issues
- Adjust priorities
- Resource allocation

### Friday: Performance Review
- Compile weekly metrics
- Team feedback session
- Improvement planning
- Next week preparation

### Content Performance
